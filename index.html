📊 Key Demographic Finding

Pakistan's fertility rate fell from 5.1 (2000) to 3.4 (2023) — a 33% decline driven by urbanization and rising female literacy. Yet at 2.0%/yr growth, the country still adds ~4.6M people annually. If fertility reaches replacement level (2.1) by 2040, population stabilizes near 280M; without this, it exceeds 350M by 2050.
